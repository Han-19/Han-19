## 🧠 About Me

I'm a physics-minded developer currently based in **Munich, Germany**.

I like building things at the intersection of **data, robotics, machine learning, and scientific computing**.  
Right now, I'm working as a **Data Analyst Working Student at idealworks**, where I get to work close to robotics and real-world industrial data.

Outside of that, I enjoy exploring:

- 🤖 autonomous systems and robotics

- 🌌 quantum science, space, and astrophysics
- 🧪 scientific computing and simulations
- 🛠️ small tools that make messy data more useful
